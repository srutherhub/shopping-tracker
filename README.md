# Shopping-tracker
A smart iOS app that helps you track and improve your grocery shopping habits by analyzing receipts and categorizing purchases. Scan receipts and visualize your shopping trends over time.


# Features
- **Receipt Scanning** - Scan a receipts
- **Categorize Purchases** - Organize items into categories such as "ingredient" "unhealthy" "vegetables" "fruits" "meat" "household"
- **Trend Data** - Visualize your spending habits overtime, track specific items overtime
